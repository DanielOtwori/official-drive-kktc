# Drive KKTC – Discover Northern Cyprus by Road

A modern tourism and road-trip discovery platform designed to showcase the beauty, culture, history, and hidden gems of Northern Cyprus through curated scenic driving routes.

## Overview

**Drive KKTC** helps visitors explore Northern Cyprus beyond the traditional tourist attractions. The platform presents carefully curated driving routes that connect historical landmarks, coastal roads, villages, monasteries, beaches, viewpoints, local markets, and cultural experiences.

The website is fully multilingual and designed to provide an immersive travel planning experience for international visitors while promoting lesser-known destinations across the island.

## Why I Made This Website

As an international **Software Engineering student**, I noticed that many visitors only experience a small portion of what the island has to offer.

Most tourism websites focus on hotels, beaches, or major landmarks, but they rarely tell the story of how everything connects together through the journey itself.

The idea for this project was inspired by **Gökhan Hançer**, **Director of Ata Bilişim**, who encouraged me to think beyond a traditional tourism website and explore how technology could be used to showcase Northern Cyprus through meaningful travel experiences. His perspective on digital innovation and local tourism helped shape the initial vision behind this project.

Inspired by that idea, I created **Drive KKTC** to encourage exploration beyond the usual tourist destinations. My goal was to design a platform that helps travelers experience Northern Cyprus through carefully curated road trips that combine history, culture, nature, local communities, and breathtaking scenery.

This project allowed me to combine my passion for **software engineering**, **web development**, **user experience design**, and **storytelling** into a real-world application that promotes a place I have come to appreciate during my studies.

From researching destinations and planning route experiences to designing the multilingual user interface and implementing the website, this project became an opportunity to apply both technical and creative problem-solving skills to a meaningful challenge.

Rather than simply listing attractions, **Drive KKTC** aims to inspire discovery, promote local tourism, and create memorable travel experiences for visitors from around the world.

**Special thanks to Gökhan Hançer, Director of Ata Bilişim, whose guidance and inspiration helped spark the idea that eventually became Drive KKTC.**

## Development Journey

**Drive KKTC** started as a simple idea: create a tourism platform that showcases Northern Cyprus through experiences rather than individual attractions.

The first stage involved extensive research into the island's geography, history, cultural heritage, scenic roads, villages, monasteries, archaeological sites, beaches, and local businesses. Instead of focusing only on popular tourist destinations, I wanted to highlight locations that are often overlooked yet contribute significantly to the island's identity.

After gathering information, I organized destinations into themed driving routes that tell a story as travelers move from one location to another. Each route was designed to provide a balance of history, culture, nature, and local experiences.

The next challenge was creating a multilingual experience that could serve both local and international audiences. To achieve this, I implemented dedicated language versions in **English**, **Turkish**, **Arabic**, and **Russian** while ensuring accessibility, readability, and proper right-to-left support for Arabic users.

From a technical perspective, the project involved building a responsive interface, optimizing navigation, improving search engine visibility through localized content, and creating a scalable structure capable of supporting future route additions and new features.

Throughout the development process, I focused on combining software engineering principles with visual storytelling. The goal was not only to build a functional tourism website but also to create an engaging digital experience that encourages exploration and helps visitors discover the richness of Northern Cyprus.
